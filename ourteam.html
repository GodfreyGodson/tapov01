Our Team

MUSSA HABIBU MONDOSHA
Acountant
Mussa Habibu Mondosha is a chartered accountant currently working as a Finance Manger with VGK Company Limited and Wazo Road Haulage Company Limited in Dar es Salaam. He holds a Bachelor degree in Business Accounting and Finance from Mzumbe University; he is a Certified Chartered Accountant and is currently pursuing his master’s degree in Business Administration (MBA) from the University of Dar es Salaam. He has experience of more than 10 years in auditing, accounting and financial consultancy. He has previously worked with Globe Accountancy Services, Claritas International, BakerTilly DGP & Co Ltd and is currently the Finance Manager of VGK Company Limited and Wazo Road Haulage Company Limited as well as being the treasurer of TAPO where he joined in September of 2022.
UPENDO AVELINA MASANCHE
Business Administrator
Upendo Avelina Masanche is graduated business administration from Institute for Information Technology Tanzania. She is currently working as an assistant Operational Manager and Business Hall supervisor at Starmedia Digital company in Tanzania. She has experience in Administration, mentoring, team management, sales, Customer service, training, quality assessment, leadership, and operation management.
Nickson Urio
Administrative Officer
Nickson is a Social Welfare Officer graduated from Institute of Social Work Dar es Salaam. He practices expertise on community serving programs and management. Nickson has over 7 years on gaining extensive experience in working with Public and Private Institutions on development programs and economic empowerment. He previously works with African Medical & Research Foundation AMREF, Muhimbili National Hospital MNH, Forestry Development Trust FDT, Masa Institute of Science and Research Tanzania MISRT, in various positions (counselor, health welfare officer, researcher, and project manager), later he joins AfyaCheckers Company and Tanzania Food and Nutrition Center TFNC, on implement Food Hygiene and Safety project as the Project Administrator. Nickson joined TAPO in May 2022, as Administrative Officer.
Emmanuel Julius Butiku
Program Field Manager
Program Field Manager Key and Vulnerable Population/DREAMS project, highly motivated, with more than ten years working experience in donor funded projects (PEPFAR/GLOBAL FUND) delivering of appropriate and high quality evidence based health care services. Very passionate especially when dealing with young girls (AGYW), Working in a thorough and committed manner, quickly adapts to new systems and procedures, able to own initiatives and work successfully as part of a team and good troubleshooter for quick achievements of intended goals through guiding principles. He has been working with Health and Medical Foundation since 2018 to date. He is responsible in supporting implementation, development of tool for data collections, M&E and reporting of DREAMS Intervention including WORTH+ package, SBCC, GBV, MCT and other biomedical services in DREAMS implementations. Emmanuel Julius Butiku holds a Bachelor Degree of Sociology (Hons) from the University of Dar-es-salaam Tanzania.
